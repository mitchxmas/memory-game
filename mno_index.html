Special Cards:
1- Select the level you want to play
2- Each player takes turns and clicks any two cards on the board to try and find matching cards
3- If a pair is found, the player scores 1pt and plays again. If no pair is found, cards are turned back over for the next player to try his luck
4- Wild Cards:
- Charity: the opponent is forced to give up 2 pairs in favor of the player holding this cardContainer
- Hero To Zero: the opponent loses all his points and his pairs are back on the board
- Crazy Shuffle: the visible remaining cards are reshuffled. ! This card can be used at any time and the player does not have to wait for his turn to use it.
